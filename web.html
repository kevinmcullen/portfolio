Music For The Arts 2017

Music For The Arts is an annual charity event in Philadelphia that raises money for a photography scholarship at The Universiy for the Arts and also benefits breast vancer research through its proceeds.

PROJECT BRIEF

I updated and re-launch a new version of the website for the 2017 event with the specific goal of making it easier for people to find the relevant information for the event, as well as improve the process making donations. I was wanted to make the site a bit more engaging and lively this year given it's 10th anniversary and its overall mission — sharing and expanding the work of artists.

Music For The Arts is fully responsive on any device

The website was built using HTML, CSS, Sass and Javascript — including jQuery. I was responsible for all design and development.
