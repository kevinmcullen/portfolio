Kevin Cullen

designer | editor

I currently am a designer and editor at The Dallas Morning News and do freelance design work. I’m an avid sports fan and design geek. I spent a year in Nashville as a sports designer frequently designing multiple products on tight deadlines. I also have a large reporting and writing background for multiple newspapers in Texas. In addition I have extensive editing experience.

I formerly was the editor-in-chief of The Daily Toreador, the student-run newspaper at Texas Tech University. I have been EIC since Spring 2009 and will graduate in December 2011. I have been working to improve my ability as a designer since I first became editor after serving as a sports reporter for the year prior to my promotion.

The majority of my design skills are more or less self taught with a few helping hands from colleagues and friends I’ve made along the way. I had two news internships during my college career, one at The Bryan-College Station Eagle and one on the metro desk at the San Antonio Express-News, both of which were fantastic learning experiences.
