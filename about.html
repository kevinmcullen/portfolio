Kevin Cullen

I am a designer and editor at The Dallas Morning News and do freelance design work. Originally from Las Cruces, N.M., educated in beautiful Lubbock, Texas, at Texas Tech University, I've called Dallas home for a little over two years now.

I’m an avid sports fan and design geek. I spent a year in Nashville as a sports designer immediately after graduating in 2011 where I frequently designed multiple products on tight deadlines. I also have a large reporting and writing background for multiple newspapers in Texas. In addition I have extensive editing experience.

I'm constantly looking to improve my ability and range as a designer and find new ways to solve problems. Please don't hesitate to reach out if you're interested in working together.
